GROUP EXERCISE

Group exercise is a form of physical activity that people do together in a group setting. It can help improve fitness, heart health, and weight loss. It can also boost mental health and create a sense of community. Types of group exercise Yoga: Can improve flexibility, posture, and mental clarity Zumba: A high-energy dance fitness class that provides a full-body workout training: A series of exercises with limited or no rest
